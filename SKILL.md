# CANLAH AI Skill

This skill integrates the core concepts of CANLAH AI into the GenPark ecosystem, providing specialized AI agents for every business function—marketing, sales, service, and commerce—while maintaining a unified brand identity through Style Genome™ technology.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Style Genome™ Brand Memory**:
   - Encodes brand identity (color palette, composition, tone, density) into a 768-dimensional memory vector.
   - Ensures all generated content perfectly matches the brand's visual language, unlike generic AI outputs.

2. **CanMarket (Marketing Automation)**:
   - AI content generation and multi-channel campaign automation.
   - Generates brand-consistent content at scale.

3. **CanArt (Creative Studio)**:
   - Text-to-image (Seedream 4.5) and smart image editing (Gemini).
   - Features 14-reference composition, poster generation, and typography.
   - Delivers professional-grade images in seconds without a studio.

4. **CanSell (Sales & Commerce)**:
   - Multilingual sales chat with intelligent upselling (+15% AOV).
   - Instant store crawling for Shopify, WooCommerce, and arbitrary URLs.

5. **CanService & CanCommerce (Support & Analytics)**:
   - Omnichannel AI chat with smart ticket routing and sentiment analysis.
   - Listing optimization, ad campaign AI, and inventory intelligence.

## Usage Guide

### Brand Configuration
Before utilizing the agents, configure the brand's `Style Genome™` by uploading reference images and describing the visual style. This memory vector is shared across all agents to maintain consistency.

### Workflow Integration
- **Marketing/Design**: Use the `CanMarket` and `CanArt` capabilities to turn single product photos into main images, infographics, lifestyle shots, and video ads.
- **Customer Journey**: Deploy `CanSell` on web chat or Telegram for instant 24/7 product knowledge, and route post-purchase follow-ups or support tickets via `CanService`.

## Integration Points
- Fully compatible with GenPark's enterprise integrations.
- Can be extended via **Agent Studio** concepts for bespoke AI agents with custom model routing and brand memory access.
